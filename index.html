How I Got Here

I studied Cultural Industry Management at Beijing Film Academy — immersed in storytelling, productions, and commercial strategy. Early on I worked at an MCN agency and in TV drama PR and distribution. Real work, but I was always at the periphery of where things were actually being made.

Then 2024 happened. AI exploded — ChatGPT, Sora, generative models reshaping every industry. I was preparing for graduate school at Tsinghua at the same time. The timing felt like a signal I couldn't ignore.

I leaned into the intersection I'd always sensed: film × technology. I trained LoRA models, built Stable Diffusion workflows, and directed AIGC projects at Enlight Media. For the first time, I wasn't just analyzing creative industries — I was building inside them.

From there, a natural progression: designing a multimodal companion product at Zhipu AI, engineering full agentic pipelines at Tianji Cloud, and now asking harder questions at USC's AI Trust Lab — how do we know if AI is actually trustworthy?

The common thread isn't film or AI. It's the question of how humans experience technology — and what it takes to make that experience trustworthy.

Chapter 05 — Independent Builds

LandIt

A preparation system, not a question generator — structured role-specific training that improves candidates across an entire interview cycle.

01
Fragmented prep — resume, research, questions, advice all disconnected. No system.
02
Shallow tools — question banks lack personalization; mock tools lack structured feedback; copilots raise ethics issues.
5-Agent Architecture
Loop Preparation Loop
  • Background Builder — persistent digital profile from resume & context
  • Role Context Agent — job-specific criteria from JD input
  • Interview Orchestrator — adaptive pacing, difficulty, follow-ups
  • Scoring Agent — 6-dimension evaluation with role-adjusted weights
  • Progress Tracker — feeds weaknesses into next simulation
OKR Success Metrics

North Star — Score improvement across 3 consecutive mock sessions

  • Mock completion rate · 7-day return rate
  • Sessions per Target Role · subscription conversion
  • Weakness convergence rate
± Tradeoffs
  • Deep personalization → modular agents + clean data flows to avoid brittle chains
  • Adaptive orchestration → balance realism with response speed
  • Video analysis deferred → prioritize structured text scoring in MVP
  • Positioning risk → must emphasize growth, not just another mock tool
